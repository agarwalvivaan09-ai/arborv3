Methodology

Financial Resilience Index (FRI)

FRI combines savings rate, debt-to-income, liquidity, solvency, and emergency coverage into a normalized 0–100 composite score.

Financial Independence Score (FIS)

FIS emphasizes solvency, liquidity, and savings as long-term independence indicators.

Stress Simulation Model

Income and expense shocks are applied non-linearly using exponential decay functions. Stress FRI measures resilience under volatility.

Monte Carlo Simulation

5-year projection using Geometric Brownian Motion with correlated stochastic shocks, recession regimes, crash probabilities, and asset volatility modeling.
